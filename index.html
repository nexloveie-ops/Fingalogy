About Us

Located in Dublin, Ireland, Fingalogy Limited is committed to delivering innovative and sustainable solutions in the IT and electronics sectors. Our mission is to integrate green energy practices into our services, contributing to a more sustainable future.

We specialize in IT and Electronics Services, with plans to expand into Battery and Energy Storage After-Sales Services, focusing on green energy solutions and sustainability.
